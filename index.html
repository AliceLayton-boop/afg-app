Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 8, 2026

Who We Are

A Friendly Game ("AFG," "we," "us") is a compatibility matching platform that helps board game players find compatible gaming partners and lasting friendships. We are based in the United States.

What We Collect

We collect the following information when you use AFG:

Your name and callsign — the first name and animal you choose during registration (e.g., "Amy the Owl"). We never ask for your full legal name.

Your questionnaire answers — gaming preferences, personality questions, values, and interests you share during the matching process.

Contact information — your email address, if you choose to provide it through our Talk to Me form or during registration.

Session data — your browser stores your session locally so you can return to where you left off.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for one purpose: matching you with compatible gaming partners. Specifically:

Your questionnaire answers are compared with other users' answers using our compatibility algorithm to generate match scores.

Your name and animal callsign are shown to your matches so they can find you at events.

Your email is used only to contact you about your matches or to respond to messages you send us. It is never shown to other users.

Your individual questionnaire answers are never shown to other users. Only your callsign, match percentage, and general compatibility highlights (like "shared values" or "thinks alike") are visible.

What We Never Do

We never sell your data to anyone.

We never share your data with advertisers.

We never use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

We never display ads in AFG.

We never share your individual answers with other users or third parties.

Where Your Data Lives

Your data is stored in a Supabase cloud database. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. Your browser also stores a local session copy so you can resume where you left off.

If you contact us through our Talk to Me form, your message is also processed by Web3Forms to deliver the email notification to us.

Sensitive Information

Some questions in our deeper tiers touch on faith, political outlook, LGBTQ+ identity, and gender preferences. These answers are treated with extra care:

They are entirely optional — you can skip the gateway questions entirely.

They are used only for compatibility scoring, never displayed to anyone.

They are deleted along with all your other data if you choose to delete your account.

Your Rights

Delete your data: You can delete all your data at any time using the "Delete My Data" link found at the bottom of every screen. This permanently removes your record from our database and clears your local session.

Access your data: Contact us through Talk to Me to request a copy of your stored data.

Correct your data: You can update your answers at any time by returning to the questionnaire through your profile.

Children

AFG is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
